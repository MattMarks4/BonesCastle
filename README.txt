Bones & Ruins is a simple game and study project i made with Unreal Engine 4 using C++ and Blueprints. 
It features a basic inventory system with usable items, some AI enemies made with behaviour trees, 
a heart-based life system, some damaging traps, interactable chests and a player character who uses 
magic to attack enemies, and a custom camera that you can zoom in and out and change through 1st and
3rd person perspective.
